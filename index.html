FIND THE RIGHT CARE FOR YOU

We know that finding the right healthcare can be a challenge, so we offer free resources and guidance to help you make informed decisions about your health.

Welcome to

Jackson Square Medical Center & Pharmacy

Welcome to Jackson Square Medical Center. Our clinic has been serving the Hamilton, Ontario community for over 10 years. We are a state-of-the-art medical facility located in the heart of Hamilton.

We offer a wide range of services, including primary care, urgent care, and specialty care. We also have an on-site pharmacy and laboratory. Our experienced and compassionate staff is dedicated to providing quality care for all of our patients.

Read More

MINOR TREATMENT

A visit to our clinic for minor treatment is the best way to ensure that your minor health concerns are addressed quickly and effectively. Our highly trained staff will work with you to understand your symptoms and provide the best course of action.

ON-SITE LAB

Our onsite lab is available for your convenience. We offer a variety of services, including blood work and urine tests. Our experienced staff will provide you with the care you need. We are dedicated to providing you with the best possible care.

MEN'S CARE

Our clinic offers comprehensive care for men of all ages. We offer primary care, mental health services, and sexual health services. We also offer specialty services such as urology, cardiology, and endocrinology. Our goal is to provide high-quality, compassionate care for our patients.

WOMEN'S CARE

We offer a variety of services such as gynecological care, family planning, sexual health counseling, and more. Our staff is dedicated to providing quality care for all women. We accept most insurance plans and offer sliding scale fees for those who are uninsured.

GERIATRIC CARE

As we get older, it becomes increasingly important to take care of our health. At our clinic, we offer geriatric care services that can help you maintain your health as you age. Our team of experienced doctors can provide you with comprehensive care, from routine checkups to more specialized treatments.

VACCINE & IMMUNIZATION

We offer a variety of vaccines, including those for influenza, pneumonia, and tetanus. We also offer immunizations for children, such as the DTaP vaccine. We know that your time is valuable, so we strive to provide quick and efficient service. Come see us today to get vaccinated!

ONSITE PHARMACY

The On-site Pharmacy in Hamilton, Ontario has a 10-15 minuite policy, which is great news for patients. This is a huge benefit for patients who need their medications as soon as possible. The On-site Pharmacy is open Monday to Sunday from 9:00 am to 5:30 pm.

VIRTUAL SPECIALISTS

Telemed MD is a virtual specialist platform that helps you access comprehensive care through using virtual online solutions and in person virtual centres across Ontario. The best part is that these appointments are often much faster than in-person appointments. You don’t have to wait for months to see a specialist.
